adyar falls information

The term waterfall could be misleading as the level f water is not too much. I understand that during monsoons the levels are higher but even then do not expect something big. You need a small trek to get to this place.

Waterfall in Mangaluru, Karnataka Road Highway Near Adyar, Mangaluru, Karnataka

Adyar Falls, nestled in the heart of Mangalore, is a charming and serene tourist attraction. While it may not be as grand as some larger waterfalls, it still holds its own unique beauty that is worth experiencing. The flowing water is refreshingly clean, making it even more enticing. To reach this hidden gem, visitors must park their vehicles at the top and embark on a 1.5-kilometer trek. One should be mindful of couples or lovers who often frequent the area.
